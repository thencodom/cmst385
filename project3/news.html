Tasha Cobbs to Release a New book

Gospel singer Tasha Cobbs Leonard, celebrated for her soul-stirring music and dynamic worship leading, is branching into the literary world with her debut book, "My Journey to Grace," slated for release in spring 2024. This highly anticipated memoir will offer fans and readers a profound and intimate look at her personal and spiritual journey.

Tasha Cobbs Leonard's career took off with her debut album "Grace" in 2013, featuring the hit single "Break Every Chain." This song not only topped the Billboard Gospel charts but also earned her a Grammy Award, solidifying her place in the gospel music industry. Over the years, Tasha has released several acclaimed albums, such as "One Place Live" and "Heart. Passion. Pursuit," which have resonated with audiences worldwide due to their powerful messages and inspirational themesâ€‹

In "My Journey to Grace," Tasha Cobbs Leonard shares her life's story, delving into the experiences that have shaped her faith and career. She opens up about the challenges she has faced, including her struggles with depression and anxiety, and how her unwavering faith has helped her overcome these obstacles. The book aims to provide readers with not only a deeper understanding of Tasha's life but also a source of inspiration and encouragement to persevere through their own struggles.

Beyond her musical achievements, Tasha is deeply involved in her ministry work. She serves as a worship leader at Relentless Church in Greenville, South Carolina, and through Tasha Cobbs Leonard Ministries, she focuses on empowering and uplifting individuals through worship, mentorship, and community service. Her openness about her mental health journey has also made her a beacon of hope for many, as she uses her platform to promote mental wellness and spiritual growth

The memoir is expected to be a heartfelt narrative that combines Tasha's life experiences with her profound insights on faith and grace. It will highlight key moments from her career, personal anecdotes, and the lessons she has learned along the way. Readers can anticipate a compelling read that is both inspirational and transformative, much like her music.

"My Journey to Grace" is set to be a significant addition to Tasha Cobbs Leonard's impactful career, offering fans and new readers alike a chance to connect with her on a deeper level. The book will be available in bookstores and online in spring 2024, promising to be a must-read for those seeking inspiration and a testament to the power of faith and resilience.
